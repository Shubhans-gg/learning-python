"      YOU F'ING DONKEY     "

Inside every human mind lives a stubborn little donkey. 
This donkey wakes up late, ignores common sense, and brays loudly whenever responsibility knocks. 
The donkey does not read instructions properly, then complains when things go wrong.
When logic whispers, the donkey shouts. When patience is needed, the donkey stomps.

This donkey loves shortcuts that lead nowhere. 
It presses random buttons, skips thinking, and then looks surprised when chaos appears. 
The donkey mistakes noise for confidence and confusion for courage. 
It charges ahead proudly, dragging bad decisions like a cart full of stones.

Whenever progress is near, the donkey sits down. 
Whenever effort is required, the donkey suddenly feels tired. 
It insists it knows everything, yet learns nothing. 
The donkey laughs at advice, trips over the same mistake, and then blames the road.

Worst of all, the donkey is loud about being right. 
It brays opinions without facts, argues without understanding, and stands stubbornly even when clearly wrong. 
Wisdom waits patiently nearby while the donkey kicks dust into the air.

The real challenge in life is not fighting others, but taming this donkey within. 
Because once the donkey is quiet, learning begins, growth follows, and the cart finally moves forward.
